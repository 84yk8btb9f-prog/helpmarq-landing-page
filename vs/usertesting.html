When to use each tool

Use HelpMarq when…

  • You're an early-stage founder or indie maker
  • You need feedback on a pitch deck, idea, or early prototype
  • Budget is zero or near zero
  • You want structured written feedback you can act on quickly
  • You want multi-perspective feedback in under 48 hours
  • You need accountability from reviewers (rated, public profiles)

Use UserTesting when…

  • You're running formal enterprise UX research
  • You need video recordings of users thinking aloud
  • You have a research budget
  • You need large-scale quantitative usability data
