Why Product Hunt doesn't give you the feedback you need

The audience is optimizing for upvotes, not helping you improve

Product Hunt is a competitive launch platform. Commenters write short, positive comments to participate in the community — not to give you honest, deep feedback. "Great idea! Upvoted." tells you nothing actionable.

HelpMarq solution: Reviewers use structured templates that require specific, substantive responses. Vague feedback gets low ratings.

You only get one official launch window

Relaunching on Product Hunt after a poor first launch is almost impossible — the community remembers and the algorithm deprioritizes repeat submissions. If your product isn't ready, you waste your only real chance at viral distribution.

HelpMarq solution: Submit as many times as you want, at any stage. Get feedback on the idea, then the prototype, then the landing page.

Product Hunt's audience isn't your customer

Makers, designers, and tech enthusiasts dominate Product Hunt. If you're building for HR managers, healthcare workers, or small business owners, the people voting and commenting have no idea what your customer actually needs.

HelpMarq solution: Choose your reviewer profile — experts, practitioners, or target users — matched to your actual audience.
