Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account on HelpMarq, we collect your name, email address, and any profile information you choose to provide. When you submit a project, we collect the project content, description, and any files or URLs you upload. When you submit a review, we collect the review text and any structured feedback data entered through the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate the platform — matching project owners with reviewers, facilitating feedback delivery, and maintaining user profiles and ratings. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising. We may use your email to send platform notifications and important account updates.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. No system is completely secure — please use a strong password and do not share your login credentials.

4. Your Rights

  • You may request a copy of all data we hold about you at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • You may update your profile information at any time from account settings.
  • To exercise any of these rights, contact info@helpmarq.com.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain platform functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect platform functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

The platform is hosted on Vercel (frontend) and Render (backend). User authentication uses secure JWT tokens. We do not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising platforms during the beta period.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days. Review content may be retained in anonymized form for platform quality purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

HelpMarq is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact info@helpmarq.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page and notify active users by email for significant changes.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at info@helpmarq.com.
