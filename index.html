The Problem

Every builder hits the same wall

You finish something. You need honest feedback. Every available option fails you.

Friends are too kind

The people who care about you don't want to hurt your feelings. They say "looks great" instead of telling you what's broken. Polite feedback is useless feedback.

Forums give nothing

You post your project online and either get ignored, get a one-word reply, or get advice from someone who's never built anything like yours. No skin in the game.

Professional feedback costs too much

Hiring a consultant or a UX agency isn't realistic at early stages. Most founders and creators are left to guess what's wrong until they've already shipped the wrong thing.

Built on Trust

Quality by design

Every part of the platform is designed to keep feedback honest, structured, and fair for both sides.

Dispute Protection

Clear dispute resolution process for both project owners and reviewers. Platform mediation available for any contested feedback or payment.

Reviewer Ratings

Every review is rated after delivery. Public rating system ensures accountability and visibility — quality reviewers rise, poor ones don't get matched.

Anti-Abuse Measures

Advanced filtering prevents fake reviews and spam. Identity verification and review pattern analysis keep the platform clean.

Structured Feedback

Reviewers work through structured templates. No vague "looks nice" — every piece of feedback addresses specific, defined areas.

Quality Control

Top-rated reviewers get priority access to new projects. System rewards consistency and penalizes low-effort or unhelpful reviews.

Transparent Process

Every step visible to both parties. Project owners see reviewer history before matching. Reviewers see clear project briefs before accepting.
